# INTRODUCTION
The Task-1 (Local Service Business Website Redesign)
Task 1 focuses on redesigning a local service business website.
For this task, I selected a salon named "ONYX" and redesigned its website to improve visual appeal and user experience.

# TARGET USERS
The target users are individuals looking for premium hair and skin care services.
These users expect a professional appearance, clear information, and a smooth browsing experience.

# LAYOUT STRUCTURE
Coming towards the Layout Structure of this website, it is designed to keep the user experience simple and easy to navigate.
The "HOMEPAGE" -- This page is where a Customer starts to build a trust and I wantwed to represent the Service " ONYX" in the most premium visuals.
The "Services" -- This Page is where the Customer wants to see when they open a Salon website and I designed this to show them an Overview of Services that the Salon provides.
The "Men Page" -- This is a sub page under "Servies" page is designed to give a preview of Servies that are provided for "MEN" in this Salon.
The "Women Page" -- This is the sub page under "Services" page is designed to give a prevoew of serives that are provided for "WOMEN" in this Salon.
The "Booking Information" Page -- This is the page from where "Appointment" flow starts where the customer selects their preffered services along with their preffered timings.
The "Booking Review" Page -- This is the page where the Customer can preview their appointment beofre confirming to pay for the booking.
The "Booking Confirmation" Page -- This is the page where the Customer will find their Whether their Booking is confirmed or not along with the Booking ID.
The " Account" Page -- This is the profile page of a customer where they can find their details for their bookings along with their Personal Info.

# UX Decisions
The UX decisions were made to ensure a smooth and comfortable browsing experience for users.
- A simple navigation structure was used so users can move between pages without confusion.
- Clear section separation helps users quickly scan and understand the content.
- Consistent spacing, alignment, and typography improve readability and visual balance.
- Call-to-action buttons are placed in visible areas to guide users toward booking or contacting the salon.
- The overall design avoids clutter to maintain a premium and professional feel.

# Conversion Improvement
The design focuses on making it easy for users to take action without feeling confused or overwhelmed.
By keeping the layout clean and the navigation simple, users can quickly understand what the salon offers.  
Important information such as services and contact options is easy to find, which reduces hesitation.
Clear call-to-action buttons guide users toward booking an appointment or contacting the salon.  
The step-by-step booking flow helps users feel confident before confirming their appointment.

# Conclusion
This project helped me understand how UI and UX decisions affect the overall user experience of a website.
By redesigning the Onyx salon website, I focused on creating a clean layout, smooth navigation, and a premium look that builds trust at first glance.  
The task improved my understanding of layout structuring, user flow, and conversion-focused design thinking.
Overall, this project was a valuable learning experience and helped me gain confidence in designing user-friendly interfaces for real-world applications.
